Sinigang

Ingredients/Prep

  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of ground black pepper
  • 2 tablespoon fish sauce
  • 1 packet of sinigang sa sampalok mix
  • 1 lb of pork neck bone, cut into cubes
  • 1 lb mustard leaf greens
  • 1 lb of okra
  • 3 long green peppers
  • 1 large red tomato with couple of cherry tomatoes, but into small pieces
  • 1 medium onion, cut into thin slices

Instructions

  • Boil 10 cups of water
  • Put tomato, onions, and pork into boiling water; let boil for 40 minutes until the meat is tender.
  • While, almost done boiling, add all seasonings and fish sauce.
  • Wait 5 minutes, and add all the greens and cook for another 5 minutes.

  • Reminder that the sour it is, the better!


Kare Kare

Ingredients/Prep

Reminder to wash all your veggies and meat thoroughly.

  • 1 teaspoon - salt, pepper, and magic sarap
  • 1 bunch of bokchoy
  • 1 lb of tripe; boil tripe for 1 hours and cut into small pieces.
  • 1 lb of string beans
  • 1 medium onion, dice it
  • 5 cloves of garlic, mince them
  • 1 cup of peanut butter (with chunks, or no chunks it is up to you)
  • 1 pork cube
  • 1 tablespoon of bagoong paste
  • 1 lb of pork or beef (in this recipe we are using pork), cut into thin slices
  • 2 long purple eggplants, cut them into lengthwise pieces

Instructions

  • Pour 3 tablespoon worth of oil in pan.
  • Sautee garlic and onion; when brown, toss in the meat and wait until color has changed.
  • Throw in your tripe, and pour 5 cups of water into the pan.
  • Throw in a pork cube, and cook until tripe is softend.
  • Cover the pan and let it boil for about 45 minutes to 2 hours (depending on how quick the pork and tripe become tender)
  • Add salt, pepper, and magic sarap.
  • Boil 10 cups of water in a pot, and boil your eggplant for about 5 minutes, then set aside.
  • Boil string beans for about 5 minutes, then blanch and set aside.
  • Boil bokchoy for about 3 minutes then set aside and drain pot.
  • In a small bowl, dilute Mamasita seasoning with 3 tablespoons of water, then add 3 tablespoon of peanut butter and mix.
  • Throw in the mixture, as well as the vegetables into the pan and simmer until sauce thickens a bit.
