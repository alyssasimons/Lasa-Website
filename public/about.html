About Lasa

Lasa is a recipe website consisting of my mom's Filipino recipes! Filipinos don't usually measure any of their ingredients accurately; they generally tend to eyeball everything. When recreating the dishes on this website, I suggest for you to create your meal based on what suits your taste whether that be adding more seasoning than instructed, or adding another ingredient to please your palette!

Recipes on this website will not only cover one ethnic group in the Philippines, but a vast range of ethnic groups dishes in the Philippines!
